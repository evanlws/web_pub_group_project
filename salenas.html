Salena's

Salena's is a family owned and operated Mexican restaurant, located in a new location at Village Gate on North Goodman Street. There are nine beers on draft and a selection of over 80 tequilas. Margaritas are made with real ingredients and no sour mix. Salena's has an outdoor bar and seating as well which overlooks beautiful sculptures and twinkle lights.

They also offer catering, in-house paint classes, and tequila tasting classes. Salena's has been voted "Best Mexican Restaurant" in the Roc City Newspaper's "Best of Rochester" awards every year from 2007-2015.
